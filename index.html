How does it work?

Our service allows you to create an encrypted image for storing confidential information.

The encryption process is quite simple. Follow these steps to encrypt:
01

Upload an image

Select any image in JPEG, GIF, or PNG format with a size of at least 1024 x 1024 pixels.
02

Write the text

Add the text you want to encrypt. The higher the resolution, the more characters can be encrypted.
03

Generate a key-password

Our key generator will help you create a secure key-password, or you can enter your own.
04

Save the result

Confirming the creation of the encrypted image and download it or copy it to the clipboard. Don't forget to save the key-password.
To view the information encrypted in the image, follow these steps:
01

Upload the image

Select the image you wish to decrypt.
02

Enter the key-password

Enter the key-password that was used to encrypt the data.
03

Confirm decryption

After entering the key-password, confirm the decryption, and you will gain access to the decrypted data.

"The image that tells your secrets only to you."
